About:

A React webapp called Topsterify that utilises the Spotify API to show a users top artists and tracks in a visual format. Users are given the choice to look at their top data from 3 distinct periods: last 4 weeks, 6 months and 12 months. The webapp is also responsive with dedicated views for different screen sizes. I worked on this project alone as music is one of my main interests and I plan on updating it with new functionality in the future.
